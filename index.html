About Me

I am a polite and reliable individual with a proven ability to work with others in high-stress environments. Working as a Porter in the A&E department necessitated me to develop many skills like following instructions swiftly and carefully and communicating with relevant staff. I have a Level 3 qualification in engineering at D*DD and possess a natural problem-solving mind.

Like alot of people my age after I finished sixth form I was confused about what career I wanted to take so I ended up just working a normal job, During the covid pandemic after seeing so many people lose their jobs, I realized that I needed a skill so that I wouldn't be dependent on my current job. I began to look again to try and find a career that I liked and eventually stumbled upon web development, At first, I was highly skeptical because of previous experiences I had with programming in my engineering course however I decided to give it another go. I quickly fell in love with it due to my natural mathematical/problem-solving mind and my keen eye for design, I was also just astonished at the sheer amount of things that could be programmed and how the industry was only growing. I now dedicate 40 hours a week to developing this skill and aspire to be a source of motivation in the programming community.
